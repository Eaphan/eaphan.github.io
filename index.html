Yifan Zhang

I am a final year Ph.D. candidate at CityUHK, working with Prof. HOU Junhui. Before that, I served as a research engineer at Baidu. I received my B.E. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology and my M.E. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My current research mainly focuses on 3D perception within the context of autonomous driving. I am open to collaborating with other researchers who are interested in related fields.

I'm currently seeking a Researcher or Postdoc position at leading research institutions, starting around June 2025. Feel free to contact me if you're interested in collaborating with me!
